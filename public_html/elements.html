Contributions and Services

Before coming to college I have always been an attendee of our local mosque in Austin, TX. When moving to College Station for the first time in fall 2017 I decided to join the volunteering team at the College Station and Bryan Mosque. Overtime I have gone from simply volunteering to be on the lead staff team where I work with other volunteers to ensure that to whoever visits us it is a comfortable and welcoming experience where they are always welcome back.

My primary functions include running organizing all kinds of various with my team for over 200+. We cater these events towards all community mebers from children to families and students! You can check out the Islamic Community of Bryan College Staion at their website or the facebook!
